Quid-e-Azam

The man who envisioned Pakistan and believed.

Nation pays tribute to Quaid-e-Azam for his untiring struggle, leadership qualities.

Here's a timeline of Quid-e-Azam's Life:

  • 1876 - Born in Karachi, Sindh.
  • 1893 - Joined Lincoln's Inn
  • 1905 - Entered Politics and joined the Congress
  • 1906 - Made his first political speech in support of the resolution on self-government
  • 1910 - Jinnah was elected to the newly-constituted Imperial Legislative Council.
  • 1916 - Lucknow Pact was made
  • 1946 - The League won most reserved Muslim seats in the elections
  • 1947 - Pakistan came into being
  • 1948 - Death of Jinnah

"We are starting in the days where there is no discrimination, no distinction between one community and another, no discrimination between one caste or creed and another. We are starting with this fundamental principle: that we are all citizens, and equal citizens, of one State."

-- Quaid-e-Azam
